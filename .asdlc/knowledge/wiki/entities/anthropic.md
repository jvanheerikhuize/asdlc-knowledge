# Anthropic

> AI safety and research company, creator of the Claude family of models and of the
> **Model Context Protocol**.

## Relevance to this cluster
Anthropic published and open-sourced [[model-context-protocol|MCP]] in
[[mcp-anthropic-2024]] (Nov 2024), an open standard for connecting LLM apps to
tools and data — directly relevant to [[agentic-memory]] and
[[context-engineering]] in [[knowledge-management-for-llms]]. Its Claude models are
also the maintainers of LLM-authored knowledge bases such as the
[[asdlc-knowledge-base]].
