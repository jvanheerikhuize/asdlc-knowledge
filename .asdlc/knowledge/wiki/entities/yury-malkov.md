# Yury Malkov

> Researcher and co-inventor of **HNSW**, the graph index that became the default
> algorithm for [[approximate-nearest-neighbor]] search in modern
> [[vector-database|vector databases]].

## Contribution
With Dmitry Yashunin, Malkov introduced Hierarchical Navigable Small World graphs
in [[hnsw-malkov-2016]] (2016; IEEE TPAMI 2018). The method's strong
recall/latency trade-off made it the workhorse behind [[semantic-search]] over
[[embeddings]], shipping in [[faiss]] and virtually every vector database. He has
also worked on machine learning at industry research labs.
