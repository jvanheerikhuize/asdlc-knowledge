# Nils Reimers

> Researcher behind **Sentence-BERT** and the `sentence-transformers` library, a
> central figure in making practical text [[embeddings]] for
> [[semantic-search]].

## Contribution
With Iryna Gurevych, Reimers introduced SBERT in [[sentence-bert-2019]] (EMNLP
2019) and created the widely used `sentence-transformers` open-source library that
turned bi-encoder embeddings into an everyday tool for
[[retrieval-augmented-generation]]. He has since led work on embedding and
retrieval models in industry.
