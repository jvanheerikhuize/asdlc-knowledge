# Akari Asai

> Researcher in retrieval-augmented NLP, lead author of **Self-RAG**, which added
> self-reflection and self-critique to [[retrieval-augmented-generation]].

## Contribution
Asai was first author of [[self-rag-asai-2023]] (2023), which trains a model to
decide when to retrieve and to judge whether its own statements are supported by
the evidence — a notable step toward reducing [[hallucination]]. Her broader work
focuses on retrieval-augmented and self-reflective language models.
