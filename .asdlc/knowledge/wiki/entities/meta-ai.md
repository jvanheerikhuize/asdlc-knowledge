# Meta AI (FAIR)

> Meta's AI research organisation (formerly Facebook AI Research, FAIR), source of
> both **DPR** and **FAISS**.

## Relevance to this cluster
FAIR produced [[dpr-karpukhin-2020|Dense Passage Retrieval]] (the dense retriever
standard) and [[faiss-johnson-2017|FAISS]] (the reference
[[approximate-nearest-neighbor]] library behind [[faiss]] and many a
[[vector-database]]). Together these underpin the
[[embeddings]]-based retrieval half of [[knowledge-management-for-llms]].
