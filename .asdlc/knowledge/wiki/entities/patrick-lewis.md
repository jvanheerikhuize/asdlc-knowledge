# Patrick Lewis

> Lead author of [[rag-lewis-2020]], the paper that named and formalised
> [[retrieval-augmented-generation]].

First author of "Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP
Tasks" (NeurIPS 2020), the work that introduced the RAG recipe of pairing a
pre-trained seq2seq generator with a retrieved non-parametric memory.
