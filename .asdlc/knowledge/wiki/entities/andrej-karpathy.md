# Andrej Karpathy

> Researcher and educator; author of the gist that originated the
> [[llm-wiki-pattern]] this knowledge base implements.

Published the [[karpathy-llm-wiki]] gist proposing a persistent, file-based LLM
wiki as an alternative to retrieval-augmented generation. The pattern's emphasis
on the LLM as a disciplined *bookkeeper* is the core idea this KB operationalises.

A practitioner report ([[llm-wiki-setup-guide-2026]]) dates the originating gist
to **April 2026** and notes it reached 5,000+ stars and forks — describing it, in
Karpathy's own words, as "an idea file, designed to be copy pasted to your own
LLM Agent" rather than a tool or package.
