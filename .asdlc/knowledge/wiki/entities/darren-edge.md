# Darren Edge

> Researcher at [[microsoft-research]] and lead author of the **GraphRAG** paper.

## Contribution
Edge was first author of [[graphrag-edge-2024]] (2024), which introduced
[[graph-rag|GraphRAG]] — running [[retrieval-augmented-generation]] over an
LLM-built [[knowledge-graph]] with community summaries to answer global
sensemaking questions. The work was released as Microsoft's open-source GraphRAG
library.
