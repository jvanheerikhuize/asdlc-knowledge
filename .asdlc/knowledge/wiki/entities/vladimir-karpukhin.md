# Vladimir Karpukhin

> Lead author of **Dense Passage Retrieval (DPR)**, which established learned dense
> [[embeddings]] as the standard retriever for open-domain question answering.

## Contribution
Karpukhin was first author of [[dpr-karpukhin-2020]] (EMNLP 2020) at
[[meta-ai|Facebook AI Research]], showing dense retrieval beats lexical BM25 for
QA — a direct precursor to the retriever in
[[retrieval-augmented-generation]]. The paper was co-authored with
[[patrick-lewis]] among others.
